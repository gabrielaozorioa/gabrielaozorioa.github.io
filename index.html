about

Born in Recife, a brazillian creative and reactive city.

I work mainly with design and human behavior, adopting psychology, neurology and perception concepts as grounding.

I love the merge outcome of architecture, urban planning, environmental design and user experience fields.

My motto is "the difference between good design and great design is intelligence" (Tibor Kalman)

Anthropological experiences, creating and adapting are my main passions.

Mail me at ozoriodegabriela@gmail.com for inquiries or a new project & download my CV bellow to see my whereabouts.
