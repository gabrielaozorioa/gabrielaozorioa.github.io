espinheiro housing

Located in the Urban Restructuring Sector 1, the Espinheiro neighborhood is classified as an ARU (urban restructuring area), with the objectives of requalifying the urban space and encouraging the coexistence of multiple uses in the territory.
This project consists of making the most of the land and its unique characteristics, offering a new concept of housing combined with public space, mixed use, and spaces to live and work.

Implantation, existing vegetation and agency.

The cropped terrain, in addiction to the current region’s legislation, limited the buildings’ implantation and format. The result is one that allows pedestrians to flow from one side to the other, since the terrain sections a block.
The first block comprises two apartment types, and the second one, 3 connected buildings formed by commercial/service use ground floor, and duplex multifunctional studios in the following ones.

Main building's floor plan. 'A' cell (at the ends) / 'B' cell (middle ones).

'A' cell zoom.

‘A’ cell’s floor plan is organized around a compact central core containing bathrooms, small hall and kitchen. The space around it can be divided by mobile panels or used as a single free span. The mobile panels are housed in cavities in the walls. The use of glass façades and the volume amplitude grant a high luminosity level into the cell. Private front garden as an antechamber to main access.

‘B’ cell’s floor plan consists of a toilet and a bath, separated, joined by the corridor. Two environments able to accomodate an intimate occupation, one of them being reversible. Balcony is also reversible.

Second block’s 1st floor plan

Multipurpose duplex studios, mezzanine or two independent floors. It is a space to live, work, and / or produce. Two of the three cells on each floor of the three mixed-use towers are connect by a suspended patio

apartment project

Interior design and makeover project for a family's apartment

When Marcela and Davi bought this apartment, it had 2 bedrooms only, and a big poorly distributed living/dining room. It used to accomodate them very uncomfortably as the furniture was in their way, and there were a lot of lost spaces throughout the floor plan.

They decided to reform their home when they found out they were having a baby.

The strategy we used consisted in resizing existing rooms (such as kitchen and social bathroom), adding a new bedroom, fixing all the balconies, furniture, grounding and walls.

The couple opted for a ceiling built-in TV in their bedroom and air-conditioning all rooms. It took around 3 months to be ready, and the outcome was a simple, organized and fluid floorplan & design, accomodating all their needs with no loss of value and space.

For more information and pictures of the makeover, please mail me @ ozoriodegabriela@gmail.com

moodboard

[before] floor plan

Analyzing the preexisting zoning, it was clear that the original floor plan was overly segmented, which tackles a fluid reading of the space by the user.

Through the redesigning and zoning distribution process of the zones seen as private, social and service, it became pertinent to encompass one preexisting bedroom and the entry toilet to the living room. The kitchen area was also incorporated to the room, seeking transparency, fluidity and a simplified floor plan.

Despite the loss of one of the preexisting bedrooms and the toilet, it was possible to create more plural environments – of different types and accommodation levels. The toilet was replaced by the old bedroom’s bathroom.

The office space was enlarged and adjacent to it a foyer is placed, in case business needs to be discussed in a less personal area. Those are close to the entrance for simple access.

Understanding that the concept of living room around television is an outdated-model, it was chosen to place a sofa oriented to the window view, the kitchen, and the dining table, seeking for integration.
Regarding entertainment, a relaxing environment was designed with a retractable screen and projector.
10 environments were incorporated where before there were 7, respecting the user's flow and offering different accommodation configurations.

destemerizadas

winning proposal @ i9 contest

The contest objective was to propose an interpretation of the domestic space that opposes the president’s standpoint, which attested that women should be astute followers of the household budget and its price fluctuations, besides taking care of the household chores and not leave little for the children’s education, only just that way increasing their presence in the labor market.

The response to this statement should be manifested through a compact and temporary architectural object, measuring 6’5” x 6’5” x 6’5” feet, which would be located nearby the university’s architecture building.
