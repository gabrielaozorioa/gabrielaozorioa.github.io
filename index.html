espinheiro housing

Located in the Urban Restructuring Sector 1, the Espinheiro neighborhood is classified as an ARU (urban restructuring area), with the objectives of requalifying the urban space and encouraging the coexistence of multiple uses in the territory.
This project consists of making the most of the land and its unique characteristics, offering a new concept of housing combined with public space, mixed use, and spaces to live and work.

Implantation, existing vegetation and agency.
